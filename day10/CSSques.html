CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) is crucial in web design as it allows for the separation of content and presentation. CSS acts as the "skin" of a website, turning plain text into a visual experience. It allows you to control colors, fonts, and layouts across your entire site from one place, ensuring your design is consistent and loads quickly. Most importantly, it makes websites responsive, so they look great on everything from mobile phones to large monitors.
